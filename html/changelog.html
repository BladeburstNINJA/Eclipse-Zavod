04 June 2021

EvilJackCarver updated:

  • Roaches' collectible hats now only have a 5% chance to spawn.
  • Roaches' hats no longer glow in the dark when dropped.
  • Hivemind should no longer be round-ending for low-population rounds.
  • Hivemind mobs' and machines' maximum health scale based on active players, with a floor of 75% and ceiling of 100%.
  • Hivemind machines' health regeneration per Process() tick now scales based on active players, with a floor of 50% and a ceiling of 100%.
  • Hivemind machines and turfs now interfere with fire alarms if there are fewer than 7 active players.
  • Assembler spawns are now restricted based on the maximum health of the spawned mob and the number of active players.
  • Fire alarms that are automatically triggered will no longer repeatedly play their audibles.
  • All drones now have a GPS tracker.
  • Phoron glass is now fireproof.
  • Increased air alarm visibility in low-light and alarm conditions.
