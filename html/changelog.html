29 September 2021

EvilJackCarver updated:

  • Partially refactored how welding fuel spreads, to account for Z-level travel.
  • Fixed an issue where welding fuel would create a stack of puddles atop each other if they fell a Z-level.
  • Fixed an edge case where a Benzin lit on fire would not explode. Now, a Benzin lit on fire will explode 3 to 15 seconds later.
  • Fixed an edge case where a Benzin could leak welder fuel more than once.
  • Increased the amount of welder fuel a defeated Benzin will leak. (Was 5, now 50.)

Sly9sky updated:

  • strange plants will no longer spawn or burrow into maintenance

23 September 2021

drexample updated:

  • Music tapes and jukebox support for them
  • New tab for people with Custom Items to equip their items from (they all cost 0 points, aside from specific outliars)
  • Barsign now supports custom sign sprites
  • Fixes save/load/selection issues of loadout items.

19 September 2021

drexample updated:

  • Removes Horizon Class bluespace drive

18 September 2021

Valanthe500 updated:

  • Changes the BulletHeaven Vending Machine's Account from the Bar to the Union.

27 August 2021

Valanthe500 updated:

  • fixed the escape pods to better reflect our server's lore.

11 August 2021

EvilJackCarver updated:

  • Added a variable to projectiles to determine if it will explode welderfuel tanks or Benzin roaches.
  • Benzin roaches no longer explode from blunt-force trauma.
  • Benzin roaches now leak welder fuel when killed.
  • Benzin roaches now gib when exploded. You can now detonate Benzin roaches after their death if you so desire. (Why anyone would WANT to do that, is beyond me.)
  • Buffed Benzin health up to 25 to counteract the above.
  • Welderfuel tanks no longer explode if shot at by bullets that shouldn't explode them; they will instead leak.

07 August 2021

Sly9sky updated:

  • Autolathes will no longer print magazines that are less than full
  • Autolathes can have their print quality restored with very-high-quality parts
